Stevens Varisty Swim Team

My swimming career began when I was ten and I started competitive swimming when I was thirteen. As part of the swim team I have taken on the roles of SAAC representative and recruitment liason. As a SAAC representative I act as a liaison between student-athletes and athletic administration, as well as the campus community. We aim to promote positive sportsmanship and leadership through community service, civic involvement, and academic excellence. A a recruiment liason for the swim team I was in charge of creating events and activites for potential student athletes when they came to visit for the weekend.
